About This Calculator

This calculator uses your age, weight, height, gender, and activity level to estimate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). It then adjusts based on your goal to recommend daily calories and optimal protein intake for muscle preservation and growth.

Features

  • ✔️ Calculates calories for cutting, bulking, maintaining, or recomping
  • ✔️ Provides recommended daily protein intake
  • ✔️ Simple, clean, and mobile-friendly design
  • ✔️ Evidence-based calculation formulas
