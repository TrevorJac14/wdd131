Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide users with a simple and accurate calorie and protein calculator for cutting, bulking, maintaining, and recomping. It will help users set fitness goals with tailored macro recommendations and explain the reasoning behind them to promote healthy and informed nutrition choices.

Audience

This site is for fitness enthusiasts of all levels who want to calculate their calorie and protein needs for any fitness goal, including beginners learning nutrition basics and advanced lifters optimizing their macros.

Dynamic elements

The calculator will use JavaScript to dynamically calculate Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) based on user inputs (age, weight, height, gender, activity level), adjust calories according to the selected goal (cut, bulk, maintain, recomp), and display recommended daily protein intake.

Content

Home page

The home page will welcome users, introduce the purpose of the site, and explain briefly how to use the calculator. It will include a CTA button linking to the calculator page and a short section about why calculating calories and protein accurately matters for fitness goals.

Calculator Page

This page will contain the input form (age, gender, weight, height, activity level, goal) and display calculated calorie and protein recommendations with explanations. It may include educational tips below the results for each goal.
